Our Courses

Course We Offer

Intermediate

Intermediate courses at the University of Jordan serve as a crucial bridge between
introductory studies and advanced specializations. Typically taken in the second and third years of undergraduate programs,
these courses delve deeper into specific topics within a student's major, building on foundational knowledge. They combine theoretical learning with practical applications, preparing students for advanced coursework and research.
Through lectures, labs, and collaborative projects, intermediate courses equip students with essential skills and knowledge, fostering academic and professional growth.

Degree

The University of Jordan offers a wide range of degree programs across disciplines such as humanities, sciences, engineering, health sciences, and business.
These programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical
experience through lectures, labs, and internships.
Committed to academic excellence, the University ensures that graduates are well-prepared for professional success
and meaningful contributions in their fields.

Post Graduation

The University of Jordan offers master's and doctoral programs across various disciplines. These advanced programs provide in-depth knowledge, research opportunities, and specialized skills, preparing students for leadership roles and professional success. With a strong focus on research and innovation, the University ensures that postgraduate students are equipped to tackle global challenges and excel in their fields.

Our Facilities

The Library

The Library at the University of Jordan offers extensive resources and facilities, providing students and faculty with access to a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support their academic and research endeavors.

Activities complex

The Activities Complex at the University of Jordan offers a variety of sports, recreational, and cultural facilities, fostering a vibrant campus life and promoting student engagement and well-being.

University restaurant

The University Restaurant at the University of Jordan provides a diverse range of dining options, offering nutritious and affordable meals for students, faculty, and staff.
